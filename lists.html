Lists in HTML

This lesson focuses on the different types of lists in HTML

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists are simply numbered lists and are used when the order of list items is important.

  1. Preheat the oven to 350oF (175oC).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and eggs in a bowl.
  3. Pour the mixture into a baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists are bulleted, not numbered and are used when the order of list items is negligible.

  • Pizza
  • Ice cream
  • Chocolate

Description Lists

Description lists pair items with their descriptions as in a glossary, dictionary, etc.

Simple Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350oF (175oC).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Place two spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

Cooking Terms

Preheat
Heat (an oven or grill) beforehand.
Bake
Cook (food) by dry heat without direct exposure to a flame, typically in an oven.
